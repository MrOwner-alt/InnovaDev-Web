Join Our Team

1. Submit Application

Send your portfolio, previous projects, or work experience through our Discord server with the role title in the subject.

2. Skill Assessment

Complete a role-specific assessment to demonstrate your expertise:

  • Developers: Build a small project or solve a coding challenge (72-hour deadline).
  • Designers: Create sample assets or redesign an existing interface.
  • Community Managers: Respond to a simulated community scenario or draft a community engagement plan.
  • QA Testers: Identify and document bugs in a provided sample project.
  • Project Managers: Develop a project timeline or risk management plan for a hypothetical scenario.
  • Marketing Specialists: Create a marketing strategy or campaign proposal for a target audience.

3. Collaboration & Onboarding

If selected, you'll join our team and collaborate on exciting projects. We'll ensure your contributions are credited and provide opportunities to showcase your work.
